My Project

Here you will find some of my personal and research projects in Android app development and antenna design, showcasing my skills and expertise in these fields.

Antenna Design

The Log-Periodic Dipole Array (LPDA) antenna has been successfully designed and simulated across multiple operational frequencies, including 433 MHz, 900 MHz, 1200 MHz, 1500 MHz, 2400 MHz, and 5800 MHz. This antenna is developed to support an anti-drone system with multi-mode and multi-frequency jamming capabilities. With its wide frequency coverage, the antenna can effectively interfere with various drone communication and control signals, enhancing the effectiveness of drone threat mitigation in diverse operational scenarios

Android App Development

This Android application is developed using the Dart programming language and is designed to assist cleaning service teams in monitoring the fill levels of trash bins. The application works by receiving data from sensors installed in the bins to detect whether they are full or not.
